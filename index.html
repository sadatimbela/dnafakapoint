ABOUT DANIEL NAFAKA POINT

MORE ABOUT

Our Company specializes in sourcing high-quality whole grains particularly rice and beans directly from farmers and selling it to regional distributors and retailers.

By cutting out the middleman, we are able to offer competitive prices while also ensuring that our farmers receive fair compensation for their hard work. Our dedication to quality extends beyond just our products. We also pride ourselves on providing exceptional customer service to our regional distributors and retailers, ensuring that they receive their orders on time and in perfect condition. Whether you are looking to stock your grocery store shelves or your restaurant's pantry, Our Company is your one-stop-shop for premium whole grains.
